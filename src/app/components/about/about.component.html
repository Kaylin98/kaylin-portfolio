About Me

Passionate Full Stack Software Engineer building robust, scalable systems

My Story

Studied Computer Science at UKZN, and most of what I've built since lives in .NET and C# — the backend architecture and APIs nobody notices until they break. Angular is the other half, turning that architecture into something people actually use.

At home, Home Assistant runs on Zigbee and YAML configs I've written and rewritten until every device actually talks to the others properly. Systems that just work, end to end, matter to me whether it's code or not.

Outside of work hours, I build games in Unity and C# — writing a rocket's thrust physics or an FPS post-processing pipeline from scratch teaches you things a work ticket never will. And most weekends, I'm watching Liverpool with more conviction than the table position usually justifies.

Education

BSc Computer Science & Information Technology — University of KwaZulu-Natal, Durban (2016 - 2019)
